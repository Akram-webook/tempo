# Book 1b — World-class questions (ask before any feature)
The orchestrator runs the relevant lenses by task size.
**Business:** Why? Who asked? ROI? Alternatives? Opportunity cost?
**Product:** Who uses it? When? Why? What pain? How often? What value?
**Engineering:** Can we simplify? reuse? automate? scale?
**UX:** Is it obvious? Could a brand-new employee use it without onboarding?
**AI:** Can AI replace / predict / automate / summarize this?
**Operations:** Who owns it? What breaks? What happens at peak season?
**Executive:** Would the COO care? Would the Director use it? Would the employee love it?
If the answers are weak, shrink or stop (Delete Framework, Article IV).
