# 01 — Product Discovery
Before building, answer these (JTBD-first). If they can't be answered, don't build yet.
- What decision does this help a leader make? (tie to `VISION.md`)
- Who asked for it, and what evidence shows the need?
- What is the job-to-be-done, in one sentence?
- What is the simplest version that delivers the decision value?
- What would we measure to know it worked? (→ `09-analytics`)
