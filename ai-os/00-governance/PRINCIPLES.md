# Principles (read before any work)

## Product principles
Tempo exists to improve operational decision-making. Never build features that increase complexity
without increasing decision quality.
People > Metrics · Fairness > Surveillance · Decision Support > Reporting · Automation > Manual Work ·
Evidence > Opinion · Operational Simplicity > Technical Cleverness.

## Engineering principles
No duplicate logic · No hidden business rules · Everything documented · Everything testable ·
Everything observable · Everything reversible.

## Design principles
Calm UI · Executive-first · Progressive disclosure · Accessibility (WCAG) · Mobile friendly ·
Dark mode · RTL · Fast.
