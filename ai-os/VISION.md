# Tempo — Vision: a Digital COO
Tempo is an AI-powered Operations Intelligence Platform that continuously observes operational
signals, analyzes workload and execution, challenges decisions, recommends improvements, predicts
risks, and helps leaders make better decisions before problems happen.

**The bar for every feature:** does it help leaders make a better operational decision? If not, cut it.

## Executive questions Tempo should eventually answer (with trustworthy data)
- Who is most likely to burn out next month?
- Which manager consistently overloads their team?
- What process creates the most delays?
- If we hire one person, where should they go?
- Which KPI is no longer useful?
- Which recurring task should be automated?
- Which events consistently exceed planned capacity?
- What skills are we missing as a team?
- Who is ready for promotion based on sustained impact and growth?
- Which meetings create little value and consume the most time?

People > Metrics. Fairness > Surveillance. Decision support > Reporting.
