# Book 19 — Metrics Bible
Single source of truth for every KPI: name · what decision it serves (Article IV) · formula · inputs ·
thresholds (green/amber/red) · alert · owner. No metric exists here without a decision it supports.
Vanity metrics are deleted (Delete Framework).

| Metric | Decision it serves | Formula (plain) | Green / Amber / Red | Owner |
|--------|--------------------|-----------------|----------------------|-------|
| Workload load % | Is this person over capacity? | weighted assigned effort ÷ monthly ceiling | <80 / 80–100 / >100 | Manager |
| Burnout flag | Who needs relief before they break? | sustained >100% load over N weeks + signals | — | COO |
| Evaluation completion | Are reviews actually getting done? | completed ÷ due this cycle | >90 / 70–90 / <70 | Director |

(Seed — extend as real metrics are defined; every row must name its decision.)
